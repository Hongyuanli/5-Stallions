Welcome to Blue Tape Society

Blue tape is at the beginning of great art...

Blue Tape Society is a group for young artists and creatives. The Society’s mission is to help aspiring artists jump-start their careers through monthly galleries that will allow their work to gain exposure among the community, creating networking opportunities that will ultimately advance their careers. So if you’re an aspiring artist, a lover of art, or just in town and itching to see what young artists in New York have to offer. Come check out our gallery in Brooklyn.
